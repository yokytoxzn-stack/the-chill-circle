# 🌍 The Chill Circle — Nairobi Outdoor & Adventure Group

Welcome to **The Chill Circle**, a Nairobi-based outdoor and adventure community created for fun-loving explorers who enjoy connecting with nature, meeting new people, and living life outdoors. 🌿  

---

## 🎯 About Us
**The Chill Circle** brings together young adventurers and social souls who love picnics, hikes, road trips, and chill hangouts around Nairobi and beyond.  
Our goal is simple: **Adventure. Friendship. Freedom.**
